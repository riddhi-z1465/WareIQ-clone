Track Inventory in Real-Time

Gain full control and visibility over your inventory with our real-time tracking system. From the moment your stock enters our fulfillment center to the second it leaves for delivery, every movement is recorded and updated instantly on your dashboard.



With real-time inventory tracking, you can:

• Avoid stockouts and overselling with automated inventory alerts
• Track SKU performance and identify fast-moving or dead stock
• Enable smart replenishment through predictive analytics
• Integrate seamlessly with your eCommerce platforms (Amazon, Shopify, Flipkart, etc.)
